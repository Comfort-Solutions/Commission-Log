Instructions

To add a log entry, click on the "Add" tab in the navigation bar. To remove a log entry, click on the "remove" tab. To view the entire log, click on "View". Remember to send the data to the office and clear the log, for that type of refrigerant, once you switch to a new refrigerant cylinder before entering in new data.
